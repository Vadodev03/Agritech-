establishing the importance of AgriTech. The core section ('L'Écosystème AgriTech') allows
interactive exploration of solutions via filters and detailed modals, which is more engaging

task-oriented structure (exploring solutions, understanding challenges) was chosen to make
the dense information digestible and to empower the user to find information relevant to
them, rather than forcing them to read from top to bottom. -->

- Report Info: Distribution of featured AgriTech companies by country. Goal:

- Report Info: Challenges vs. Strategic Approaches. Goal: Relationships/Compare. Viz:
